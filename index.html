About me

I'm a (soon to be) PhD researcher at the Geometric & Visual Computing (GeoVIC) group at École Polytechnique in France. Working as part of the CLIPE ITN project funded by the European Union, I'm looking into the applications of Multiagent Reinforcement Learning in the area of Crowd Simulation, following up on my previous Master's work about ad-hoc cooperation in MARL via Theory of Mind.
